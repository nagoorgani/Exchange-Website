FAQ

Common questions

How long does an international transfer take?

Most transfers to our major corridors complete within minutes; some destinations may take up to one business day depending on the payout partner.

What documents do I need to send money?

A valid Emirates ID or passport is required for individual transfers, along with the recipient's full details.

Is Al Dahab Exchange regulated?

Yes, Al Dahab Exchange is fully licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of the UAE.

Can I open a corporate WPS payroll account?

Yes, our corporate desk can set up WPS-compliant payroll processing for businesses of any size — contact us to get started.
